Mom had baked twelve cookies. Now there were only ten. Who was the thief? Tom and his sister Mia looked for clues. They found a few crumbs near the back door and a small, muddy paw print on the floor. Just then, their dog, Buster, trotted in with chocolate on his whiskers and a very happy tail. While walking on the beach, Chloe found a beautiful conch shell. When she held it to her ear, she didn't hear the ocean; she heard a faint, tinkling song. She was sure it was magic! Her grandpa looked at the shell and smiled. He showed her a tiny, hidden music box tucked deep inside. It was a gift he had made for her grandma years ago and lost on this very beach. The shell wasn't magic, but finding it certainly felt like it.
